Sravillit Tcoss

I am Sravillit Tcoss, a rising rapper and producer. I am from the South Bay and I've been writing rhymes since I was 13. I'm currently pursuing my music career as a solo artist, and I write and produce my own music. I am also a member of the 7th & Ithaca group. We've released our debut album 'Pay Day' on September 24th, 2018. I'm originally from Compton, CA. I was born in 1983. I've been a part of a lot of groups and I've done a lot of different things, but my main focus is rap, write lyrics and produce beats. A young, black, queer, feminist, and trans woman, also the first rapper to have an album called 'Savage Love' that was all about sex.
